Step-by-Step Usage Guide

1

Cleanse Your Face

Start with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt, oil, and makeup. Pat your face dry with a clean towel.

2

Apply Vitamin C Serum

Take 2-3 drops of serum on your fingertips. Gently pat and spread evenly across your face and neck, avoiding the eye area.

3

Wait & Absorb

Allow the serum to absorb completely for 10-15 minutes before applying any other products.

4

Moisturize & Protect

Follow with a moisturizer and always apply SPF 30+ sunscreen during the day as Vitamin C can increase photosensitivity.

Pro Tips

  • • Start with once daily application, preferably in the morning
  • • Store in a cool, dark place to maintain potency
  • • Perform a patch test before first use
  • • Can be used with niacinamide but avoid mixing with retinol
