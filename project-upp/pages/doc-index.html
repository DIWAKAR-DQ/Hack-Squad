FAQ

How do I book an appointment?

Select a doctor, choose a time slot, and complete the payment to book your appointment.

How can I book an appointment with a doctor?

Go to the "Appointments" section, choose your doctor, select a date & time, and confirm your booking.

Can I consult with a doctor through chat or audio only?

Yes, Nexus Medihub provides both chat box and audio consultation options to connect with doctors.

What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

Use the Emergency Call option on our homepage for instant connection to our helpline.

Can I buy medicines or health products directly from the website?

Yes, you can use the Shopping section to purchase medicines, supplements, and medical equipment.

Is my personal medical data secure on Nexus Medihub?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and comply with healthcare privacy standards.

How do I know which doctor is right for my health issue?

Use our Doctor Specialization Guide in the appointment page to choose the right specialist.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled at least 24 hours before the scheduled time.

Do I need to create an account to use the services?

Yes, a simple free registration is required to access appointments, shopping, and chat support.

Are prescriptions provided after online consultation?

Yes, doctors can issue digital prescriptions which will be available in your account.

Can I contact a doctor anytime?

You can send a message via chat anytime, but doctors respond during their available hours.
